Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the spinal tracking?

Our clinical sensors maintain a <0.5mm margin of error in static environments, validated against traditional goniometric standards.

Can I export data to EMR systems?

Yes, PostureAI supports FHIR standards and direct HL7 integration for most major electronic medical record systems.

What happens if a sensor disconnects?

The system uses local cache to maintain session integrity and automatically reconnects via encrypted Bluetooth 5.2.

Is there a patient-facing mobile app?

Absolutely. Patients can access their "Posture Journey" app to follow prescribed exercise protocols at home.

Still need clinical support?

Our kinesiologists and technical leads are available for 1-on-1 consultations to help integrate PostureAI into your specific workflow.
